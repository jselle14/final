Relax, Explore, Enjoy

Nestled in a beautiful valley in the Swiss Alps, our resort is the perfect escape from the mundane. Dining options include an Italian restaurant, and ski-in bars on mountainside terraces. Other amenities consist of an upscale spa, hot tubs, a gym and a climbing wall.

Amenities and Options for Everyone

Escape from everyday life and spend a few warm and relaxing days with us. Enjoy the refreshing mountain air and explore the village and surrounding mountains. Visit our spa or ski runs during winter, or take a guided hike in the warmer months.

An Escape For Any Time of Year

Whether you prefer snowy mountains or peaceful summer getaways surrounded by lush valleys, we have it all. Hit the slopes in winter, or enjoy hiking, picnics, and swimming in the spring and summer.
